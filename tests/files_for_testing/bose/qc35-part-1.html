Overview

Replace lost or damaged ear cushions for your QC35 headphones with a new pair. Easy to install, they form a critical acoustic seal to give you an extra level of quiet.

Compatible products

  • QuietComfort 35 wireless headphones II
  • QuietComfort 35 wireless headphones
Rated 5 out of 5 by Joe from Good longevity, straightforward replacement My original ear cushions lasted about 2.5 years of daily use before they started to break down from normal wear and tear; I ordered these replacements, and they work just as well as the originals, and were relatively easy to install. I’m glad that these are a replaceable part — it would be a shame to have to replace the entire headset! Ease aside, I had a couple of minor hiccups on installation. 1) the instructions say there are 9 hooks that the pads need to clip under. My QC35 II had 10. Something to watch out for to ensure a good seal. 2) I used a spudger to force the edge of the pads under the hooks — this could probably be done with a fingernail as well, but it might be worthwhile to include a small tool for this?
Date published: 2020-04-27
